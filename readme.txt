=== Support Occupy Wall St Banner ===
Author: Jeff Couturier - http://jeffcouturier.com/occupybanner/
Tags: wordpress plugin, occupy-wall-street, occupy, banner, protest
Requires at least: 2.8
Tested up to: 3.2
Stable tag: 1.2

Places a simple banner saying "I Support the Occupy Movement" on your site.

The banner itself links back to the banner project page with information and 
links on the Occupy movement, and instructions for getting and using the banner. 
The goal here is to make it super easy for people to put this banner on their 
sites and thus spread awareness and participation in the Occupy movement.
View the project page: http://occupybanner.wordpress.com
